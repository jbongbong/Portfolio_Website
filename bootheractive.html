Project Description

Bootheractive functions as an entertainment booth with its purpose to increase social interaction along the walkway between Changi City Point and office buildings. There is an opportunity to develop in an area since there is no other form of entertainment in the relatively long walkway. It is targeted at office workers for them to interact with other workers and to uplift their moods after a long day at work. Not only does it bring life to their mundane and repetitive lifestyle but the concept of social interaction embedded in the project also makes it meaningful.
